Food Oasis

What is a food desert?

A food desert is defined as a geographical area that lacks grocery stores with a 1 mile radius in an urban setting and 10 mile radius in an urban desert

What is being done to tackle food desert

  • Food policy council to better food distribution
  • Getting convenience stores to sell healthier foods
  • Farmer’s market
  • Subsidized supermarket.
